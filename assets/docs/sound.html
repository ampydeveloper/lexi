sound()

Sounds are a big part of coding in Lexi. A sound is a short snippet of audio. You can play any sound from Lexi's library, and you can control its volume and the number of times it repeats.

  • sound(NAME, VOLUME, REPEATS)

Every time you call this command, the sound will layer on top of any other sounds that are playing. You can add a volume number between 0 and 100, or a number of repeats if you want the sound to play more than once in a row.

    Plays a bird chirping.
    Plays a lion sound at 50% volume.
    Plays a frog sound at 25% volume, 4 times.
